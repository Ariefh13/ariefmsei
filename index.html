ABOUT ME

My name is Arief Hidayatullah, and I am an ambitious entrepreneur passionate about education and technology.

I am the founder and president of Cetta, an EdTech startup that helps thousands of Indonesians improve their foreign language skills to build better careers and brighter futures. I started Cetta with a vision to create a world where Indonesians have the language proficiency to compete globally. Over the last 4+ years, I have worked hard to grow the company into a thriving organization with 500+ teachers, 50+ full-time staff, and 3,000+ of monthly students.

Currently, I am pursuing my Master's in Entrepreneurship and Innovation at the University of San Francisco. This experience is helping me develop the skills and knowledge needed to scale Cetta further and introduce innovative solutions to education in Indonesia. My ultimate goal is to become a high-impact entrepreneur who inspires change and empowers others to reach their full potential.

In my free time, I enjoy teaching, mentoring, and sharing my journey to motivate others. My story is a testament to resilience, hard work, and the belief that education can transform lives.
